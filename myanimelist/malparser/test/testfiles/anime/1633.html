Shintaisou: Kari or Princess 69 was brought to us by Pink Pineapple and adapted from the 2001 Eroge by Panda House. Usually the H-Anime adaptions of eroge make them more vanilla than the source material but Shintaisou Kari is the opposite instead going over the top, adding more fetishes and just being darker all round.

So with that being said yes this is a very perverted H-Anime to start with lets mention some of those fetishes like rape, sexual torture, bondage and scat to name a few. I want to get that stuff out early so those who are turned off by such don't waste their time watching this.

The story.. well there isn't much to it big surprise, the majority of the episodes time is given to the sex which is exactly what I want anyway. Still the plot serves it's purpose. The premise keeping it brief is rich girl Tomomi and P.E. teacher Nikusuke work together for their own individual messed up reasons to find and initiate girls into the dark gymnastics club which is some kind of twisted perverted sexual game to them. Each episode focuses on bringing in new girls, breaking them down through extreme sexual acts and converting them into being part of the team. These scenes mainly take place in the school gym with the girls in gymnastics uniform and they are quite flexible, that's about the extent of the gymnastic theme. The characters are all fairly attractive which is nice, my favorite though would be the main Tomomi Shirakawa and likewise her scenes of which she's probably the only character that deserves what she gets.

Shintaisou Kari being from the early 2000s the animation can leave a bit to be desired but I think Murakami's art style is strong enough that it's still visually appealing and the lighting used gives it a distinct style, the girls are all animated great especially in the sex scenes where it counts most. Teruaki Murakami is at the helm here so If you have enjoyed any of his other works you are more likely to enjoy this one. This also means that yes the usual classic seizure inducing visual effects are present which I love, along with all the fantastic camera angle positions.

This is one of few dark H-Anime to be licensed (By Media Blasters) and therefore that nasty censoring removed which is good, however the detail under that censoring is lacking probably because the creators may not have bothered to put much detail into what was originally not going to be seen, who knows? Can't complain really it's still better than pixelization but even that stopped bothering me long ago.

Because this was licensed means we also get English dub for those who want it. Personally I think the English voice acting is pretty bad like it usually is, the girls moans just sound laid back and boring in comparison to the original. I recommend sticking to the Japanese audio if you want the voice acting to match the insanity of the animation. Apart from the voice work all the sound design here is outstanding, considering that there is more going on than just basic sex a lot of the time you will hear little details like the pressure of ropes tightening etc. The soundtrack too is fitting and sits comfortably in the background for the most part coming out strong in the right moments.

To recap there are four episodes all together, no particulary weak ones and all are packed with intense hot sex scenes. I recommend it to you if you like to get extreme with your fantasy H-Anime viewing. Over all I really like Shintaisou Kari it's one of my favorites for sure and I hope this short review gave you some insight on whether or not to give your time to the series.

I feel that I should also mention that there is the sequel Shintaisou: Shin or Princess 69: Midnight Gymnastics but it is drastically different and doesn't hold a candle to Kari in my opinion. Thanks for reading!
